About Dada Bhagwan

Dada Bhagwan was a spiritual master who introduced the world to Akram Vignan — a direct path to Self-Realization. His teachings focus on inner peace, non-violence, and liberation while living a normal worldly life.

Core Teachings

Self Realization

Know who you truly are beyond the body and mind.

Non-Violence

Freedom from hurting others through thoughts, speech, or actions.

Inner Peace

Remain untouched by worldly circumstances.

Liberation

Attain moksha through awareness and right vision.

Spiritual Quotes

“Where there is ego, there is suffering.”
